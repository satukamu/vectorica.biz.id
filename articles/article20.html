Energy Systems

Creatine & Brain ATP

Creatine monohydrate is a primary fuel source for the brain. It acts as an energy buffer, providing phosphocreatine to recycle ATP during periods of intense cognitive demand.

Buffering Neural Fatigue

The brain consumes 20% of the body's total calories. Creatine ensures this high-demand organ always has access to the "emergency fuel" required for heavy-duty processing.

Creatine Cognitive Benefits:

  • Reasoning Mastery: Improving information processing speed.
  • Short-Term Memory: Enhancing the brain's "RAM" capacity.
  • Neuro-Protection: Buffering stress from sleep deprivation.

DNA Repair Efficiency

By improving the cellular energy environment, creatine provides resources for efficient DNA repair across all major tissue types. It is one of the safest and most efficient tools for human potential.

Ultimately, creatine is about biological power. By ensuring your brain has a reliable source of energy, you allow your systems to operate at their absolute peak for the long term.
