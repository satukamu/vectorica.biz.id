Bio-Optics

Blue Light Mitigation

Human biology is deeply intertwined with the visible light spectrum. Our hormonal systems have evolved to respond to specific wavelengths at specific times, a process now disrupted by digital lighting.

The 480nm Interference

Blue-frequency light (480nm) is the signal for alertness. While beneficial in the morning, exposure after sunset suppresses melatonin production, delaying the body's primary repair hormone release.

Intervention Strategy Nodes:

  • Amber Filtering: Blocking high-frequency blue after 8 PM.
  • SCN Anchoring: Seeking bright sunlight during the first hour.
  • Digital Hygiene: Using f.lux or red-shift software protocols.

Hormonal Protection

Excessive blue light disrupts the production of growth hormone and testosterone. Managing this bio-optical friction is essential for protecting the hormonal architecture and ensuring the highest vitality.

Ultimately, mastering your light environment is about protecting your biological tempo. By aligning exposure with your evolutionary blueprint, you ensure systems operate at peak efficiency.
