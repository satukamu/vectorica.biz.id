Immune Ops

The Science of Adaptive Immunity

The human immune system is the ultimate internal defense architecture. Improving your "Adaptive Immunity" is about training this system to respond more effectively to a diverse range of stressors.

The Gut-Immune Axis

At the center of the immune system is the gut microbiome, which houses roughly 80% of our immune cells. A healthy gut is the foundation of a resilient defense.

Immune Reinforcement Metrics:

  • Vitamin D3/K2: Regulating immune cell gene expression.
  • Zinc/Quercetin: Providing the ammunition for T-cells.
  • Deep Sleep: Re-calibrating the immune recognition system.

Stress and Suppression

Chronic stress, through the elevation of cortisol, suppresses the activity of our natural killer cells. Sleep is the "security update" period for your immune system, where cytokines are produced to fight infection.

By implementing an adaptive immunity strategy, you are turning your biological defense into a pro-active and highly efficient system, ensuring your biology is always ready for environmental threats.
