Mastering Circadian Rhythms

The human species evolved under the rhythmic cycle of light and dark, a primal tempo that governs almost every biological process. This internal clock is an architectural masterpiece of biological timing.

The Light Mastery Protocol

Early morning sunlight is the most powerful signal for the Suprachiasmatic Nucleus (SCN). It sets the clock for the entire day, pre-programming the release of melatonin 14 hours later.

Daily Light Requirements:

  1. Morning: 15-30 mins of direct sunlight.
  2. Afternoon: High-intensity ambient light exposure.
  3. Evening: Blue-light mitigation (Orange/Red light).
