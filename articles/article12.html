Fasted Cardio Protocols

Fasted cardio is a strategic exercise protocol performed without calorie intake. When performed correctly, it forces the body to bypass glucose and tap directly into adipose tissue stores.

Lipolytic Efficiency

In a fasted state, insulin levels are at their lowest baseline. This absence of insulin allows for lipolysis—the breakdown of fat into fatty acids—to occur with far greater efficiency.

Fasted Optimization Nodes:

  • Morning Focus: Capitalizing on low glycogen baseline.
  • LISS Energy: Keeping heart rate in aerobic fat-burn zones.
  • MCT Bridge: Optional lipid fuel to sustain intensity.

Metabolic Flexibility

Consistent fasted cardio improves mitochondrial versatility. This is a key architectural goal, providing a stable energy baseline that is untethered from the constant need for exogenous carbohydrates.

Implementing a Vectorica-style fasted cardio protocol turns your body into a master of its own energy reserves, unlocking levels of endurance previously thought impossible.
